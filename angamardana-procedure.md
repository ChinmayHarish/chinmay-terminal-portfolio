# Angamardana Procedure (Isha Foundation)

*Transcription from source documents detailing the sequence of bodyweight exercises.*

---

## A. STANDING PROCESSES
1. **Stretching Upward** ...................................... 3 Cycles
2. **Bending Forward** ........................................ 3 Cycles
3. **Bending Forward/Legs Crossed** ........................... 3 Cycles
4. **Bending Forward/Feet Apart** ............................. 3 Cycles
5. **Bending Sideways/Feet Apart (6 Hand Postures)** .......... 3 Cycles
6. **Stretching Sideways/One Arm Raised** ..................... 3 Cycles
7. **Stretching Sideways/Clasping Wrist** ..................... 3 Cycles


## B. SQUATTING PROCESSES
1. **Straighten Leg to Front** ................................ 3 Cycles
   *Note: "Into and Out" breathing indicated.*
2. **Straighten Leg to Side** ................................. 3 Cycles
3. **Straighten Both Legs and Stand** ......................... 3 Cycles
4. **One Leg Stretched to Side, Up on 2 Hands** ............... 3 Cycles
5. **One Leg Stretched to Side, Up on 1 Hand** ................ 3 Cycles
6. **Jumping** ................................................ As many as you can
   *Note: "exhale" on jumping.*
7. **Push Ups (Emptiness)** ................................... Until your breath settles
8. **Balasana (Child's Posture)**


## C. SITTING PROCESSES
*Note on 1-3: "Into with inhale, come out exhale"*
1. **Single Leg Raised in Front** ............................. 3 Cycles
2. **Both Legs and Arms Raised in Front** ..................... 3 Cycles
3. **Legs Straightened Holding Big Toes** ..................... 1 Cycle
4. **Legs Apart Bending Sideways (6 Hand Postures)** .......... 3 Cycles
5. **Veerasana** .............................................. 5 Minutes


## D. LYING DOWN PROCESSES
1. **Knee to Forehead** ....................................... 3 Cycles
2. **Rocking** ................................................ 3 to 5 times
   *Note: "no breathing pattern"*
*Note on 3-5: "Into exhale, come out Inhale"*
3. **Chair Twisting** ......................................... 3 Cycles
4. **Spine Curved Twisting** .................................. 3 Cycles
5. **45-degrees and Legs to Side Twisting** ................... 3 Cycles
6. **One Leg to Side Twisting** ............................... 3 Cycles
7. **Knee to Elbow** .......................................... As many as you can
   *Note: "no breathing pattern"*
